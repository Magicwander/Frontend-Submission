Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete your profile with your skills and experience, connect your crypto wallet, and start browsing projects or posting your own.

We support 50+ cryptocurrencies including ETH, USDC, DAI, USDT, and all major DeFi tokens. Payments are processed through smart contracts for maximum security.

Our smart contract escrow holds funds securely until project milestones are completed. Payments are released automatically when both parties confirm completion, ensuring fair and secure transactions.

Freelancers pay 5-10% service fees depending on their plan, while clients pay 3-5% project fees. Fees are only charged on successful project completions. Check our pricing page for detailed information.

If issues arise, our dispute resolution team mediates between parties. We review project requirements, deliverables, and communications to reach a fair resolution. Most disputes are resolved within 48 hours.

Our web platform is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. A native mobile app for iOS and Android is currently in development and will be available soon!
